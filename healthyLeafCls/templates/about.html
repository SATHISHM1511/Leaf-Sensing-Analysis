About Our Project

The Leaf Disease Detection & Classification system is designed to identify plant health by analyzing leaf images. Using advanced deep learning models, we classify leaves into categories such as Healthy or Unhealthy, helping farmers and researchers make informed decisions.

Key Features

- Real-time leaf classification using deep learning.

- High accuracy detection with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and YOLOv5.

- User-friendly interface for easy image uploads and results.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide an efficient and reliable tool for detecting plant diseases early, improving agricultural productivity, and supporting environmental conservation.
